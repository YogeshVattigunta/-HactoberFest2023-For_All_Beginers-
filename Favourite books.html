About Hacktoberfest

Hacktoberfest is a month-long celebration of open source software. It encourages developers to contribute to open source projects and make meaningful contributions to the community.

Get Involved

To participate in Hacktoberfest:

  • Sign up on the official Hacktoberfest website.
  • Contribute to open source projects by submitting pull requests on GitHub.
  • Make four valid pull requests to earn cool swag and support open source.

Join the Community

Connect with fellow developers, share your progress, and learn more about open source by joining the Hacktoberfest community.
